About.

Mark is a data analyst and visualization designer working in a senior role with Glanbia's Centre of Excellence for Business Intelligence & Data Analytics in Kilkenny, Ireland. He has been visualizing data since 1988, when he and Gareth Ashe received a highly commended award at the Aer Lingus Young Scientists exhibition for a data driven project about the effects of air pollution on photosynthesis.

Before joining Glanbia, Mark worked as an architect for fifteen years in Paris and Dublin, successfully delivering buildings with multi-million Euro budgets as a design team lead and project manager. He is a former member of the Royal Institute of the Architects of Ireland and the National Register of Architects.

Mark uses RStudio, GitHub, Hadoop, Tableau and Python on Mac OS & Windows operating systems and his CV is available to view here.
