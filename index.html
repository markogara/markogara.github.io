Work.

1. Customer distribution mapping (2018; Google Maps API, kepler.gl)

A series of one-off map visualizations designed for print to explore the geography of customers and their proximity to various distribution locations in North America. Data assembly & data cleaning in R; point geocoding with Google Maps API; mapping visualizations built in Uber's open source kepler.gl project.

2. Point of sales gap analytics (2017—2019; R, Hadoop, Tableau)

A dashboard and series of interactive visualizations developed to analyze weekly product gaps using data provided by a large North American brick & mortar retailer. Automated weekly data ETL using RStudio Server & Hadoop; visualizations and maps using custom parameter calculations delivered to business users through Tableau Online.

3. Product constituent analysis (2016; R, Tibco Spotfire)

Sample aggregated views of interactive visualizations developed as part of a project to examine seasonal correlations between product constituents at a supplier level. Data assembly, cleaning & analysis in R; interactive visualizations delivered to business users in Tibco Spotfire.

4. Overall Equipment Effectiveness (2015; R, Tableau)

A series of interactive visualizations developed to analyse overall equipment effectiveness (OEE) at one manufacturing plant producing a range of materials across multiple shifts. Data assembly, cleaning & analysis in R; interactive visualizations delivered to business users in Tableau.

5. Product portfolio analysis (2014; R, Tableau)

Extracts from a series of interactive visualizations developed to cluster and analyze SKU performance across a range of product and geographic groups. Data assembly, cleaning, clustering & analysis in R; interactive visualizations delivered to business users in Tableau.

Other Work.

6. Petrol forecourt pricing in Ireland (2015 personal project; R, Tableau)

Regression model of the 2015 divergence between oil (€/barrel) & petrol (€/per litre) prices as compared to the historic relationship from 1985—2014. Oil pricing data from Eurostat; petrol forecourt pricing data from theaa.ie; visualizations developed in Tableau.

7. Food visualizations (2012 personal project; VectorWorks, Photoshop)

Samples from a series of visualizations drawn in Nemetschek VectorWorks to compare nutritional values of recommended serving sizes across a range of fruit, vegetables, meat and snacks.

8. Interface elements (2013 personal project; VectorWorks, Photoshop)

Icons, buttons, & dials drawn in Nemetschek VectorWorks with grain, texture and shadows added in Adobe Photoshop. A larger collection of these images was featured on the Control Panel blog in 2014.

9. Photography (2016—2019; iPhone)

A few landscape photographs taken in Ireland, Europe & North America — more images on Instagram.
