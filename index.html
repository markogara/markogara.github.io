Work.

1. Customer mapping (2019)

A series of one-off data visualizations designed for print to explore the geography of customers and their proximity to distribution centres. Customers are shown on these maps as blue points with distribution centres in orange. Customised maps were created for each sales director in North America showing the location of their individual accounts.

Data assembly & data cleaning in R; point geocoding using Google Maps API; map visualizations built using Uber's open source kepler.gl project.

2. Point of sales gap analysis (2017—2019)

An interactive application developed in Tableau using custom built parameter calculations to analyse product gaps in physical stores using daily point of sale data provided by a large North American brick & mortar retailer.

Automated data ETL written in R and orchestrated through RStudio Server & Hadoop; interactive application delivered to business users through Tableau Online.

3. Product constituent analysis (2016; R, Tibco Spotfire)

Sample aggregated views from a series of interactive visualizations developed as part of a project whose aim was to examine the seasonal variation of raw material constituents and to investigate correlations between these constituents at a supplier level over time.

Data assembly, cleaning & analysis in R; interactive visualizations developed in Tibco Spotfire.

4. Overall Equipment Effectiveness (2015; R, Tableau)

Sample views from an interactive application developed as part of a larger project to analyse Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE = Uptime x Performance x Quality) at one manufacturing site producing a range of materials across multiple shifts.

Data assembly, cleaning & analysis in R; interactive visualizations developed and delivered to business users using Tableau Online.

5. Product portfolio analysis (2014; R, Tableau)

Sample views from a series of visualizations designed to analyse the performance of materials across a range of product and geographic groups, with the aim of clustering similar products by geography.

Data assembly, cleaning & analysis in R; interactive visualizations developed and delivered to business users using Tableau.

Other Work.

6. Petrol forecourt pricing in Ireland (2015 personal project; R, Tableau)

Regression model of the 2014/2015 divergence between oil and petrol prices when compared to the historic relationship of these two variables from 1985—2014. While the model is consistently linear, a collapse in oil prices during 2014 was not reflected in lower petrol prices due to a combination of a weak Euro and high levels of fuel taxation in Ireland.

Oil pricing data from Eurostat; petrol forecourt pricing data from theaa.ie; regression model and data visualizations developed in Tableau.

7. Food visualizations (2013 personal project; VectorWorks, Photoshop)

Sample data visualizations from a series developed to compare the nutritional value of a recommended serving size across a range of fruit, vegetables, meat and snacks. Each visualization represents a food, each coloured bar represents a food component, and the height of a bar represents its proportion of a day's recommended allowance.

Visualizations drawn in Nemetschek VectorWorks
